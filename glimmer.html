About

The Glimmer TV is a speculative communication & assistant TV system created to ease the global crisis of Covid-19. It is designed to be used in hospitals to improve a patients mental health and quality of life whilst in hospital.


Using Glimmer, patients would be able to access a variety of free applications that would help them to pass time while staying in hospital.
