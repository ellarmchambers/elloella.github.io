About

Digital wellbeing is a movement that aims to rebalance our relationship with technology. Mobile phones are increasingly addictive with apps and algorithms getting better and better at keeping us scrolling.


Current digital wellbeing apps don’t actually prevent the user from spending lots of time on their phone, timers are easy to extend or delete and there isn’t any consequence for going over your pre-set time limit.


etu is a mobile app that helps users have a healthier relationship with their phone and prevents social media addiction by helping to create a more conscious user.
