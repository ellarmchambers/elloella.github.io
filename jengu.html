About

Jengu is a speculative project that tackles some of the difficulties that patients with diabetes face. It provides users with an application that helps them track and manage their medical conditions, erasing the need for paper diaries.


Its tracking features allow GPs to monitor their patients' health and communicate any concerns they may have. Jengu would ease the lives of patients and eliminate some of the stress that comes with managing diabetes.
