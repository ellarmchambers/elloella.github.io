About

Jengu provides patients with the ability to track and manage their medical conditions, erasing the need for paper diaries.


Its tracking features would allow GPs to monitor their patient’s health and communicate any concerns they may have. Jengu would ease the lives of patients and eliminate some of the stress that comes with medical conditions.
