My Projects

Below are a few of the independent projects that I have worked on both at University and during my time as a freelance website designer. I thoroughly enjoyed working on each one and look forward to creating many more. Click on each project to learn more.

The Ethical Social Media Project

Created for my final year dissertation, this project looks at the effect that social media usage may be having on a user’s mental health, and provides solutions to some of the most problematic features.
Jengu

A speculative project created in my second year of university that required us to design a mobile & web application that tackled a real-world issue based around health and wellbeing.
Emma Whyte

A website project that started in my very early years of freelance work. During that time I have been in charge of the design & development of various projects from Emma Whyte, we still work together regularly.
Etu

Created for a module in my final year of university that was based around internet based systems and contemporary development methods, this was my first project that looked at a user's mental health.
Emma Turner Interior Design

A project that began in late 2021 after Emma Turner had started the initial steps on creating her own website and wanted a helping hand in bringing her plans and visions to life.
Glimmer

This project was a result of a module in my final year of university. The module asked us to create a speculative system that addressed the global crisis of Covid-19, another project that allowed me to look at mental health & wellbeing.
