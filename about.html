About Me

A passionate UX Designer with a love for technology & creativity. I graduated in 2021 with a Bachelor of Arts in Internet Design with First Class Honours and was awarded the ‘BA (Hons) Internet Design Best Project’ for my dissertation project on ethical social media.

I am currently working at a small digital marketing agency as the Head of Web & UX where I get to work with a close knit team on a variety of projects.

Ethical Tech

I’ve always had a keen interest in technology and a fascination for how it is ever evolving, most recently I have been looking at how technology can be used alongside UX design to improve wellbeing. As a result of my work on the concept of ethical social media, I was featured in O’Reilly Radar’s article 'Ethical Social Media: Oxymoron or Attainable Goal?'.

My goal is to be a part of the movement working towards ethical design & development and hope to create products or services that take care of users and their mental wellbeing.
