About Me

My background

I grew up surrounded and intrigued by technology, I have strong memories of watching my brothers on the family computer just itching to have a turn.

In my late teens I wasn’t sure what my ‘calling’ was going to be. During my gap year and whilst working as a teaching assistant I started to learn how to code and began my exploration of the digital design space.

It was then that I found my love for creating online experiences and explored web based degrees. While in my final year of my Internet Design BA I realised I could combine my passion of wellbeing and love for UX to contribute to the ‘tech for good’ space.

My skills

In my current role I get the opportunity to utilise my skill set in a variety of ways.

As a user experience designer I use empathy to create strong user journeys and personas, allowing me to develop well thought through wireframes that enable users to reach their goals as efficiently as possible.

As an Elementor developer, I have built a variety of WordPress websites to provide clients with platforms that not only reflect their vision, but allow them to flourish.

Always learning

I firmly believe in the importance of continuous development and always seek ways to challenge myself. With every website I create, I aim to push myself and make each build better than the last. I have recently applied this mindset to my personal life by learning to play the acoustic guitar. Before this, the last instrument I played was the recorder when I was five years old!
